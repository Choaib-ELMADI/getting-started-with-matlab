> save fileName.mat             ==> Save your Workspace to fileName.mat
> delete fileName.mat           ==> Delete fileName from your Current Folder
> clear                         ==> Clear your Workspace
> load fileName.mat             ==> Load data from fileName to Workspace
> clc                           ==> Clean your Command Window

> t = linspace(x, y, N)         ==> Goes from x to y in N steps
> y = randn(N, M)               ==> Creates a N*M matrix of random numbers
